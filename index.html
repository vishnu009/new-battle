Welcome to Battlebook!

Welcome to our community. Battlebook is a social networking and community site for tabletop wargamers and role players.

Our team (which is lead by a gamer) wanted something new to share with the community, and as such we have developed Battlebook from scratch. We didnt want to use a template or an 'out-of-the-box' community framework, and as such we have developed something new and unique.

Providing all the usual social networking features such as; friends, videos, blogs, groups etc. The site is being expanded to include many industy specific applications and gaming aids, including: This Paint or That Paint, Pro-Finder, Batreps etc

Visit the Battlebook Forums to participate or find out more information about the featues we are developing, have planned or to suggest new features that you believe the community would enjoy.

Thanks again for visiting Battlebook, we trust that you will enjoy your experience and we look forward to interacting with you and the rest of the community in the future.
